Controls

Editor

Use the toolbar at the top to place blocks. In order to make a level, you'll need to place the Spawn and at least 1 Moon.

You can rotate the camera with Ctrl + Left Click or with Right Click.

When you are done, click the Play button in the top right corner!

In Game

Move around with W A S D.

Jump with Spacebar.

Collect every Moon to win!

P.S.: Every 10 Coins you gain an extra life!
